Hobbies and Fun Stuff!

Welcome to my fun page! While I'm passionate about coding and crafting digital solutions, I'm equally enthusiastic about exploring the analog world and pursuing various hobbies. Here, you'll get a glimpse of the multifaceted individual behind the code. From concerts, biking, hiking, traveling, and kayaking to cooking, thrifting, reading, painting, and crocheting, these activities keep me inspired and balanced.

Outdoor Adventures

Being outdoors is my gateway to exploring the world beyond lines of code. From traversing the Warren Dunes Hiking State Park along the shore of Lake Michigan, to paddling across Paw Paw River in my kayak, to just staring at the clouds from my airplane window seat, I thrive on the freedom and the beauty of the great outdoors. Traveling to new destinations fuels my curiosity and allows me to discover diverse landscapes and cultures.

Whether it's summiting a challenging peak, navigating pristine rivers, or embarking on long-distance bike rides, these adventures not only test my physical limits but also provide moments of serenity and connection with nature that recharge my spirit.

Passions and Pursuits

Concerts fuel my passion for live music. The thrill of discovering new sounds and sharing those musical experiences is an adventure in itself. On quieter days, you'll often find me crocheting (though I'm quite bad at it), weaving threads into intricate designs that mirror the beauty of life's tapestry. It's a craft that allows me to channel creativity and produce cozy, handmade treasures.

I'm also an avid reader (and nature lover considering I enjoy reading in nature), constantly drowning in the pages of talented women like Taylor Jenkins Reid, Jasmine Guillory, Bianca Sparacino, and Colleen Hoover. Lastly, my love for thrifting is a treasure hunt like no other. Scouring secondhand stores and markets for unique finds is not only environmentally conscious but also a journey of nostalgia, where each discovery has a story to tell. Together, these hobbies paint the canvas of my life, adding vibrancy, creativity, and a sense of exploration.
